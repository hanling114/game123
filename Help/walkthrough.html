General Tips

  • In general wander around and talk to anyone who will speak to you.
  • Some events are timed, as in they will appear, a phone will ring, a knock on the door, and you have a certain number of actions to respond. This varies from one to ten actions.
  • Some events are timed based on the number of times you visit key locations, these are commonly The Wild Ranges, Your Bedroom, The Library and a follow-up event will happen after a few visits to these key locations.
  • You can leave any item in your own bedroom.
  • The game will give you items like Old Stones at times but generally only if you do not have one in your inventory. It is best to use Stones when they are found by visiting the Wild Ranges.
  • The exact order of some events are not required, you can charm one person then another or do the second one first, but there may be an optimal order to see all possible content in the game.
  • The game saves automatically every morning and evening, but it's better to save regularly too.
  • Check the notes in your phone, it will remind you of things you have been asked to do.

Game Settings

The settings can be changed from your phone. Most of them can be found under the "App" icon:

  • Alarm: Choose when you want to wake up after a night of sleep.
  • Alert New SMS's: The game shows or hides SMS alerts.
  • Explicit Filter: The game shows or hides sexually explicit images. This can also be set at the beginning of the game.
  • Theme: Switch between white and black themes.
  • Inventory: The items in the inventory can have icons shown for the actions you can select, or text labels.
  • Inventory: The inventory can be shown in the right bar, or as a popup called from the left bar.
  • Spell Runes: You can change from the "Rune Matching" system for learning spells to the older version of looking up text (spell names) in the Book. This can also be set at the beginning of the game.
  • Undo: Enable or disable rollback on some game-ending actions. This can also be set at the beginning of the game.
  • Bubbles: The pop-up bubbles many conversation responses appear in are by default centered in the window. They can also be positioned at the lower left of the screen.
  • Memory: Changes the limit on number of save games and photos in your phone
  • Speaker: Enabled or disables most sound effects in the game.
  • Columns: Alters the layout of the center space on the screen. Using 2 columns allow text and buttons to wrap better but some players dislike the button alignment at times.

The remaining settings can be found under the "Play a game" icon:

  • Cheat Helper: Enables or disables cheats
  • Puzzles Helper: You can enable or disable puzzles. In the walkthrough the answers to the puzzles are given. Each has a variation and each is given. If you disabled them then you automatically get the puzzle correct. This can also be set at the beginning of the game.

You can also minimize the left and right bars to show a minimal set of icons. Some of the icons will show useful information if you hover your mouse over them.

Note on an Android device you can swipe left or right to open/close the side bars.

Cheating

The game has a built-in cheat mode that is enabled by the 'Cheat Helper' app in the Games menu in your phone. This enables the cheat menu which is accessible in the Left Bar of the game, at the left of the game window, clicking the app automatically opens the left bar.

You can cheat to add money or mana and this is completely safe and will not affect the story and event progression. You can also add experience to learn trainings but it is recommended you do not do this until later in the game.

Otherwise in the menu:

  • View People: Allows you to edit details for yourself or other people in the game. Includes add/remove items from a person.
  • View Items: Allows you to add or move items in the game that are not in someones possession.
  • View Places: Allows you change flags for a place. Flag 1 indicates a place is known and visitable.
  • Wait x: Allows time to pass, up to 1 day, or even go backward in time.
  • Taxi's: By default taxi cabs are available some of the time. Click the link to make them always available, click again to return to sometimes.
  • Explicit: You can toggle on/on Explicit images.
  • Locale: You can swap between US and UK setting.
  • Mod: You can toggle the mods available in the game, recommend do not use this!.

Details on flags and other technical details are documented in the files in the folder "Development".

Paths

These are major ways to play the game that considerably vary events in the game. There are three currently, some with variants.

Apprentice Path

In this path you become Sir Ronald Gates' apprentice. In this path no killing will take place, outside of a few bad endings.

Conspiracy Path

In this path you refuse Sir Ronald Gates' apprenticeship. Parts of the game are significantly reorganized, notably for money and the Clairvoyance spell. In this path no killing will take place, outside of a few bad endings.

Murder Path

In this path Sir Roland Gates is killed and you are the prime suspect and can be the actual killer. In this path people can be killed, either by accident or design.

Hard Murder Path

You demand Sir Ronald give you the Book, and argue over the book with Sir Roland and kill him by accident.

Soft Murder Path

You demand the Book but leave before killing Sir Ronald, but then witness another person killing him but you will be considered the murderer.
Death can still take place in this path, you are just not personally responsible for Sir Ronald's death. Maybe others, just not his.

Day and Night

Time passes in the game at the rate of 10 minutes per action outside and 5 minutes inside. Some actions take no time like checking items or your phone. Options to relax for a while take 1 hour or 30 minutes, depending on the location you are in.

Most shops open at 8AM and close at 6PM, but some places differ to this like the School and Restaurant.
Some events are unique to day time or night time.

Additionally there are days of the week, with the game starting on a Monday. Some places are not open on weekends, for instance the Town Hall and the Bank. Some events only happen on certain days of the week, the game does tell you these criteria and can be noted in your phone in the calendar.

You can spend the night in your bedroom, waking at 6AM. You can change your alarm setting in your phone to 6AM, 7AM or 8AM, using the Alarm App. Some friendly (read "charmed") people will allow you to spend the night at their home too.

Some things you may wish to save for night time, like using Stones or having sex again with charmed people. At night the spell Pass can be your friend and allow entry to some places otherwise locked.

The following guides do not note spending the night and some of the steps listed below can vary where you leave some for the night time.

Spells

  • Charm (Dai Chu): Increases another's desire and friendliness towards you, but only if the victim is not under the influence of someone else. A person's name is important in all forms of magic targeting a person and is a key part of casting this spell. This is a forbidden spell. Costs 10 mana.

  • Invisibility (Dest): Invoking its power cloaks the user from sight - but only sight - so one must be careful to not call too much attention to oneself while no longer visible to the naked eye. Costs 5 mana.
  • Pass (Serphoni): Lets you pass through doors and other entryways. Costs 2 mana.
  • Possession (Unstr Hun): Transports a spirit into another body, but only for a brief period of time. Costs 20 mana.
  • Shielded Charm (Neo One): Your knowledge of the shielded charm allows you to cast this spell when in public. Costs 10 mana.
  • Teleport (Shio Stin Mur): Transports you anywhere you know about but there are some limitations where you can actually teleport to. Costs 1 mana.
  • Transform (Al Mass): A spell of physical transformation. Costs 20 mana.
  • Unlife Enspelled (Mir Daru): It will bind the undead to the caster, making them the casters thrall. Unlike the charm spell the being's name is not essential to the success of this spell. If you do know it then the effect is significantly more powerful. Costs 10 mana.

Other spells are in the Book but cannot be learned at this time

Trainings

As your character gets more experienced you can learn improvements to your magical skills/spells. These are learned based on a point system.

You earn 3 experience points per spell learned and 1 point for every person/being charmed. You start with 1 point once you pick up the book, and Tess's research and the Gold Worm give a small amount. Once you learn of the "Mirror of Souls" you can look in it and see the current total of experience and total of experience spent so far.

Every 9 points you get the option to learn a training and the Book will start blinking in your inventory. Examine it. You can then consult with either Sir Ronald or Tess Adams to learn a new ability. On the Conspiracy Path Sarah can also teach you. You only need to examine the book once, the book will blink each time you get enough points. One hypnotic ability (key to Mother Superior) is learned separately from Mr. Beasley; you still need the points to learn it. Another ability, "defending yourself with magic", is learned from Alison once you charm her.

The trainings are:

  • Manipulative charms: Add variations to the charm process for selected people. You can change the outcome at any moment in the game for 5 mana.
  • Defending yourself with magic: Protection from other magical attacks if you have 20+ mana. (only learned after charming Alison)
  • Masculine cantrips: Ability to charm men.
  • Medium powers: Ability to charm ghosts.
  • Molding as desired: Addiotinal options for the transform spell.
  • Precision transitions: Can teleport with people and can inscribe your own hexagrams.
  • Moulding as Desired More options for the transform spell, including reversing transformations.
  • Conserving magic: Spells cost 10% less mana, rounded up.
  • Harnessing mana: You get 20% more mana from Stones.
  • Golden gestures: You get 50% more money from the Wealth spell.
  • Unseen: Invisibility lasts longer.
  • Firmer Possessions Possession spell does not end when you pass though a doorway. Only when you will it or at dawn or dusk.
  • Augmented hypnosis: Instant hypnotic induction. (only learned from Mr. Beasley)

NOTE: Masculine charms, Defending yourself with magic, Precision transitions, Augmented hypnosis and Medium powers are necessary in order to progress the story. Learn them, in this order or with some minor variations, as soon as you can. The walkthrough will tell you when to learn which training in order to progress with the story. The other trainigs can be useful, but are not strictly necessary in order to progress with the story.

Visit the school library. When you have the Silver Ring use it to free her. Return the next day and see a book with her. For a simple process charm her as a lover or a slave! You can also visit her home on Kollam St after you ask about it.

Alternate:

Do not use the charm spell, but talk to other teachers about her and the book, mostly important is Principal Reagan (she must be charmed). After talking to her go and talk to Ms. Titus (charmed or free slave) and ask about the book. Now visit Ms. Titus's home and read the book.

You now need to know hypnosis and Mr. Beasley's augmented hypnosis technique. Once you do visit Heather in the library and use it (a button is provided). Heather is now your hypno-slut! She can be triggered in the library, or visited at home in slut mode.

Charm Penelope

After charming Principal Reagan talk to her about school administration. She will message you in a day or so and you can now visit Penelope in the admin office at school. Charm her.

Charm Ursula

After meeting Sarah spend the night not at home. Tracy will message you. Return home and check your bedroom.

Go and talk to Angela and the Mayor at Town Hall

Go to Ursula's home on Parkview Rd (access from the Mansion). You can just talk to her and charm her as a slave or bimbo, or you can bring her to a captured Davy to charm her as a lover.

Charm Zali

Two days after transforming Miku she will tell you about a lonely cat lady. If you choose any of the options of being uninterested in the following Zali is removed from the game.

Follow Miku in the day time, and visit. Immediately charm Zali for lover type charm

Leave and decide to call the police first. Call Chief Batton and charm her as a slave

Charm Kellie

After you charm Miss Logam visit the Park bridge and meet her. Try to charm her and gail

Visit Miss Logan and discuss Kellie. Return to Kellie and talk about the quiz.

Visit the school at night (using Pass spell) and meet Kellie. Charm her, slave is the default, if you have "Manipulative Charms" you can charm her as a girlfriend.

There is different post charm content for the two paths, a bit more for the lover path.

Charm Lauren

Generally it is best to not do this! To do so the simplest way now is wait to visit Sarah in her room and do the diversions with Lauren like workout or go swimming. Each time cast the charm spell on her. Repeat until charmed!

BUT everytime you visit Sir Ronald after this he may discover it and end your apprenticeship and bar you from the Mansion forever. You can avoid by not visiting his study if wanted.

Charm Ms. Charles

After charming Mrs. Robbins and Tina and Tracy and the Mayor are freed from Davy, at least 3 days after charming Geraldine/Mrs Robbins she will say she is returning to work Monday to Wednesday

Visit Geraldine at work and ask to see Ms. Charles. Try to charm her and also cast Clairvoyance. There are now two alternatives,

  • - break the lamp and charm her. Only slave is possible here as she is angry with you.
  • - Do not break it, ask Geraldine where she lives and visit her at home (near the mansion). Can charm as slave or lover. as you wish.

Charm Jade

Vampiric Goul

Visit Jade with Lilith following. You will have to visit twice as you first need to talk to Jade about vampires in general. After visiting with Lilith talk to Lilith about Jade.

  • Visit Jade again and try to charm her, it will fail
  • visit again the following night to see a laundry event and realise she works part time in the gym
  • visit the gym between 6pm and 9pm and see Jade working there. Try to charm her again (shielded charm required), it works better but still fails
  • Talk to Lilith and she will tell you she can take Jade as a ghoul
  • visit the gym outside while Jade is working there. Talk to Lilith and choose the ambush option
  • she is now a ghoul

As a ghoul she can no longer manage the club, see below for the slave path, but talk to Lilith/Jade at the crypt to start arranging a replacement

Charmed Slave, Charmed Equal or Uncharmed Equal

Common to all three paths here, first take care of Legion. If you do not defeat Legion then only the slave option is available. Similarly you need the training manipulative charms for the others.

Visit a place of power like the Sacred Clearing or the Hidden Room if the Dragon Gem is moved there. Cast a spell, simplest is just cast Clairvoyance and you will see it is more powerful

Once Legion is defeated follow the process to free Seraphina, but once you get possession and before freeing her do the following. If you free Seraphina without Jade or have already you can still charm Jade but only the slave option is available.

  • talk to Jade and offer to show her the ritual of return
  • Do the Ritual to free Seraphina in the Hidden Room OR pretend to at the Wild Ranges
  • when you get the process preparing for the ritual call Jade to join you. She will arrive
  • do the ritual. It can fail if you mess up the setup, if this happens you can only use the slave charm
  • cast the charm spell before she leaves

The slave charm proceeds now with no other requirements. You will tie Jade up in her room at the club and she will remain there.

If you succeeded in freeing Sera you can also charm Jade as an equal, a charmed lover path

If you suceeded and are carrying the silver ring you can free Jade from the spell and she will become an equal, a lover sort of path. Different options than the charmed lover case.

Charmed Slave or Ghoul Manager

As a slave or ghoul Jade cannot manage the club or be a dominatrix anymore. Visit the club next time it open for an event telling you this.

Now visit Jade (and Lilith if a ghoul) and discuss this. Now visit one or more of

  • Mrs Robbins (charmed). She will work all week if the only one or otherwise Thurs-Fri
  • Seraphina (charmed or free). She will work all week if the only, otherwise Mon-Wed or Sat-Wed depending
  • Nella (charmed). She will work on the weekends
  • Betty (charmed lover). She will work on the weekdays

Only 3 are needed at most, you cannot assign more, their schedules will vary a little depending on the exact combinations.

Chapter 9: Ending The Game

Charm all police, including Gina and Diane, all people at Town Hall, including Emily (Tammy and Savanna are not needed). Also a few other key people like Tess, Mr Beasley. Defeat Davy, Legion and Kurndorf. You must also of met Miku.

If Jessica is bound in the cellar, make sure to choose to keep her permanently or free her by bring Hannah to alter her bindings

Return home to your bedroom and the end game should be offered.

If you already refused to end the game, prior to charming Didi, you can talk to Tess on a Sunday night to see the final sequence of the game.

A suggestion: save just before ending the game. The scene presented are chosen at random, so you'll probably see something different every time. You can choose to fully explore the ending and see all possible versions.
